如何启动mnesia cluster

1. 通过start-db.sh来启动mnesia nodes:
   start-db.sh dbnodemaster '"/home/woomsg/mnesia/dbnodemaster"'
   start-db.sh dbnodeslave1 '"/home/woomsg/mnesia/dbnodeslave1"'
   start-db.sh dbnodeslave2 '"/home/woomsg/mnesia/dbnodeslave2"'
   start-db.sh dbnodeslave3 '"/home/woomsg/mnesia/dbnodeslave3"'
   start-db.sh dbnodeslave4 '"/home/woomsg/mnesia/dbnodeslave4"'
